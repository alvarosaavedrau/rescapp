Share the log file on the Forum - Rescapp - Documentation
  • Step 1

    Rescapp searches for log files, that have been generated, by other Rescapp options.
  • Step 2

    You are displayed a list, of the files found:

    Please choose a log file

  • Step 3

    Rescapp asks you some questions to improve the post.

  • Step 4

    You are shown the log file.

  • Step 5

    You are asked to choose from several predefined forums (ubuntuforums, ...).

  • Step 6

    E.g. Ubuntuforums website is open.

  • Step 7

    E.g. Sign-in in Ubuntforums website and copy-and-paste the open text contents there.
    • Step 8

    You are informed about the share log on forum process.

    You either get:

    [SUCCESS] The log file was shared (forum) OK! :)

    or

    [ERROR] The log file sharing (forum) went wrong! :(
